bluesky.plans.run_wrapper¶

bluesky.plans.run_wrapper(plan, *, md=None)¶

Enclose in ‘open_run’ and ‘close_run’ messages.

Parameters:
  • plan (iterable or iterator) – a generator, list, or similar containing Msg objects
  • md (dict, optional) – metadata to be passed into the ‘open_run’ message
