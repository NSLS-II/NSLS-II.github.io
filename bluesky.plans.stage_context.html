bluesky.plans.stage_context¶

bluesky.plans.stage_context(plan_stack, devices)¶

Stage devices upon entering context and unstage upon exiting.

Parameters:
  • plan_stack (list-like) – appendable collection of generators that yield messages (Msg objects)
  • devices (collection) – list of devices to stage immediately on entrance and unstage on exit
